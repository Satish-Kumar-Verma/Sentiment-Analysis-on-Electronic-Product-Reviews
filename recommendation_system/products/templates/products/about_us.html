About Us

Learn more about our data extraction process and sentiment analysis methodology.

Data Extraction

Our primary source of product review data is Flipkart, one of the leading online shopping platforms. Using our custom-built extract_data.py script, we are able to efficiently extract and save valuable user review data onto our local machines.

This script navigates through product pages, captures user reviews, and systematically stores this data. Our extraction process ensures that the data used for sentiment analysis is relevant, accurate, and up-to-date.

Sentiment Analysis with Flair

Sentiment analysis plays a crucial role in understanding the general sentiment of our user reviews. For this, we utilize Flair, an advanced framework that provides state-of-the-art results in various natural language processing tasks.

Through Flair's sophisticated algorithms, we can determine whether a given review has a positive or negative sentiment. This enables us to provide meaningful insights into customer preferences and product performance.
